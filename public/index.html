Origin & Soul

From the palms
of Tozeur.

The Deglet Nour date — meaning "finger of light" — has grown in the Tozeur oasis for thousands of years. Harvested by hand every October, these dates carry a natural caramel sweetness unlike anything else on earth.

Our partnership with Tozeur farmers ensures fair wages, sustainable harvesting, and a direct supply chain with zero middlemen.

The Process

From palm tree
to your table.

Every step of the Deglora journey is intentional — from hand-harvesting in Tozeur to your personalised bottle arriving at your door.

Step 01
Hand-harvested in Tozeur

Every October, Deglet Nour dates are picked by hand from the palm groves of Southern Tunisia at peak ripeness.

Step 02
Slow-cooked into jam

The dates are stone-milled and slow-cooked at low temperature to preserve natural enzymes and complex flavours — no additives, ever.

Step 03
AI blends your formula

Our intelligence engine matches your biology, goals, and lifestyle to the ideal granola composition.

Step 04
Bottled & shipped to you

Your personalised blend is portioned into a premium borosilicate glass bottle and delivered within 48 hours.
